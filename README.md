## About Me
I recently graduated from Virginia Commonwealth University with a degree in computer science as a first-generation Latina in tech. A lot of my approach to development has been shaped by both creativity and adaptability. I love building things that feel thoughtful and polished while creating an effortless connection between the tool and the user. 

I tend to approach technical projects creatively, but in a way that still stays grounded in problem-solving and the goals of the project itself. For me, creativity and logic naturally work together. Being creative helps me think outside the box when solving problems. Allowing me to make a product more cohesive and intuitive without losing sight of what users or clients actually need. 

Outside of tech, I love creative writing, reading, cooking, dancing, and traveling. I've also participated in work exchanges while traveling, which gave me the opportunity to easily adapt to new environments and meet people from different backgrounds.
